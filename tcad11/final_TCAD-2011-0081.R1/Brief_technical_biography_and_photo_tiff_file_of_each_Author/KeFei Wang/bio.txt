KeFei Wang received the B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from National University of Defense Technology, ChangSha, HuNan, CHINA in 1994, 1997 and 2001. He is an Assistant Professor with the School of Computer, National University of Defense Technology, ChangSha, HuNan, CHINA. His research interests are in the areas of high-performance computing with emphasis on high speed interconnection.
