JianMin Zhang received the B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from National University of Defense Technology, ChangSha, HuNan, CHINA in 2001, 2003 and 2009. He is an Lecturer with the School of Computer, National University of Defense Technology, ChangSha, HuNan, CHINA. His research interests are in the areas of formal method with emphasis on unsatisfiable core extraction.
